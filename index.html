about

my name is spencer kuzara. i am 26 years old and have been involved with computers and computer technology almost my entire life. when i was 8 years old, i wanted a computer for my birthday - my father had to think about it. being the forward thinker he is, instead of buying me a computer, he bought me parts. in the box that came in the mail on the day of my birthday was a processor, ram, hdd, motherboard, etc. and if i wanted a computer i would have to build it myself. at that point i began a journey that's lasted another ~20 years (and i've got long way to go!). i've taken every networking/programming/graphic design class that was offered to me and recently i've sought to further my education in computer programming by starting at free code camp. this webpage is a compilation of my projects done either inside the free code camp program, or on my own.
